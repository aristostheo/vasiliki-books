About Vasiliki Tsatsou

Vasiliki Tsatsou is a writer, teacher, and mother of two, with a lifelong passion for history and storytelling. Born in Germany and raised in Greece, she has called Toronto, Canada, home since 2016. With a degree in History and Archaeology, she's especially inspired by ancient Greek mythology, a rich source of wisdom and imagination.

As a teacher, she continually looks for ways to make these myths come alive for young learners, blending the timeless lessons of mythology with a deep enjoyment of each story. Her goal is to create a meaningful connection between readers and the powerful messages of the past.

In her writing, she loves taking readers on journeys into magical realities. Whether it’s a heartwarming Christmas story, an adventurous romance, or a tale of life’s surprising paths, each story invites readers into an enchanting world of discovery. She hopes you enjoy exploring these pages and joining her in the joy of storytelling!
